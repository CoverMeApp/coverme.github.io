Overall look for Cover Me
===============================

The look for Cover Me is going to be a friendly warm feeling to make users feel comfortable when using our website. We are going for darker greens and teals while not using any vibrant colours.

Colours
========
The colours we are using are

- #FFFFFF
- #EEAA9B
- #1D699B
- #72B7E5
- #DBBDDB


Iconography
===========
We are focusing on round flat icons to make them blend into the webpage


Typography
==========
We will be using Open Sans font.


Links
=====
Our links will be buttons


Buttons
=======
Buttons will be rectangular with round edges to give them a sleeker style
