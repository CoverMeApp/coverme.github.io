The Big Idea

It's no secret that group activities are always hard to deal with. Whether it's as big as organizing a trip to Cuba with some friends or getting your team to meet up for a soccer practice, we all know the struggles of having to manage it all. With Coverme, your days of running after people for money, struggling to get a hold of the participants and trying to schedule an event that works for everyone, are finally over.

The basics of the our app cover your banking and social needs. You can:

- Create groups for events and invite participants

- Easily access your friends' contact information

- Use our e-transfer feature for easy e-transfers

- Communicate!
